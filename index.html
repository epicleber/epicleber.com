Welcome! My name is Cleber Vinicius Brito dos Santos. I’m currently a doctorate student at the Instituto de Medicina Social . I work with Cláudio José Struchiner, and Guilherme Loureiro Werneck, and my current doctorate project focuses in infectious disease epidemiology, specifically we aim to evaluate and optimize vaccine trials.

I did my MSc in the same institute and supervisor. In there we estimated the causal effects of deforestation on the occurrence of Visceral Leishmaniasis in the São Paulo state (Brazil).

Previously, in my bachelor I was an intern in the Epidemiology Division of the Recife Secretariat of Health (Brazil), during the triple epidemic of dengue, Zika, and chikungunya. I am particularly interested in infectious disease epidemiology, more precisely in the following themes: development and evaluation of vaccine trials, causal effects of deforestation in infectious disease emergence, and development of quantitative methods for describing the dynamics of transmission of infectious diseases.
